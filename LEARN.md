A curated showcase of the best community-made material for learning AipeHub —
videos, talks, tutorials, and writeups. **Start here if you learn better by
watching than by reading docs.**

This is the **reach analog of the [flagship template
index](docs/FLAGSHIP-TEMPLATES.md)**: flagship templates are the best things to
*remix*; the entries below are the best things to *learn from*. Curating
someone's video or tutorial here is a concrete, visible act of recognition for
the work of carrying the project to people — the other half of [pillar ⑤ of the
Recognition
System](docs/RECOGNITION-SYSTEM.md#pillar-5),
alongside [`CONTRIBUTORS.md`](CONTRIBUTORS.md). Every entry credits its author.

## The bar (we curate quality, not view count)

A piece earns a place here if it is:

- **Accurate to the current design** — it teaches the North Star right (the
  framework does not run the LLM; state is files; credentials stay local;
  federation is peer-to-peer). A polished video that gets the trust model wrong
  does more harm than no video, and will not be listed.
- **Genuinely useful** — a newcomer comes out of it able to do something they
  couldn't before: stand up a hub, write a workflow, connect a federation link.
- **Watchable / readable end to end** — finished, not a teaser.
- **Clearly attributed** — we credit and link the author.

We **do not** rank by views, followers, or referral counts. A 200-view video
that explains federation correctly beats a 50,000-view one that gets the
north-star wrong. This mirrors the rest of the system: pillar ① ranks templates
not people; pillar ⑤ curates *what's worth learning from*, not audience size.

## How to submit

1. **Open a pull request** adding an entry under the right section — yours or
   someone else's. Use the entry format in the `<!-- comment -->` under
   [Videos](#videos).
2. **A maintainer checks it against [the bar](#the-bar-we-curate-quality-not-view-count)**
   — accurate, useful, finished, attributed — and merges (lazy consensus, see
   [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md#how-a-change-lands)).
3. **Get credited.** A piece listed here also earns you a 📹 / 📢 / ✅ / 📝 row
   in [`CONTRIBUTORS.md`](CONTRIBUTORS.md). Being curated here and being recorded
   there are two distinct acts of recognition.

## Honest boundary

- **We link and credit; we don't host or pay.** Inclusion is editorial
  recognition that *this specific piece* teaches AipeHub well — not an
  endorsement of everything its author says or sells elsewhere.
- **Entries can be removed.** As the design moves, material goes stale. We will
  drop an entry that no longer teaches the current design accurately, with a note
  — keeping a wrong tutorial listed would mislead the exact newcomers this page
  exists to help.
- **No vanity metrics on this page.** No view counts, no "trending," no ranking
  by reach. The showcase records *what is worth learning from*, full stop.
